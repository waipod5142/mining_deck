The Challenge

Where mining margin is most at risk

The biggest risks in a mine are operational and rarely visible until they cost money — in downtime, damaged equipment, and safety incidents.

⏱️

Unplanned downtime

A lost bucket tooth in the crusher or a stalled haul cycle can halt production for hours.

🚧

Safety incidents

Blind-spot collisions, edge roll-overs and fatigue events cause injury, fines and shutdowns.

📉

Low utilisation

Trucks waiting on excavators and uneven dispatch leave expensive assets sitting idle.

🌫️

Harsh conditions

Dust, low light and unmarked roads defeat generic sensors and standard safety systems.

Each risk is addressable with the right mining-specific sensing and dispatching layer — purpose-built for these conditions.
